## 1. Format playbook — which / when / why

### REELS — the reach engine (~60–70% of output)
- **Which:** 30–90s vertical; voice/original-audio for API publish; in-app post for any trend-audio reel.
- **When:** default for discovery and top-of-funnel — anything you want strangers to see.
- **Why:** Reels get ~1.36× the reach of carousels and 2.25× single images (Buffer, 4M+ posts) `[well-documented]`. Reels up to **3 minutes** are now recommended to non-followers, and avg watch time doubled YoY to ~8.5s (Metricool, 24.4M posts) `[well-documented]`. Retention through the first ~8–10s is the real reach lever; tight 60–120s explainers now reach strangers, not just followers.
- **The gate:** outcome-first hook in frame 1 (the #1 hook type in OpusClip's verified 34,635-clip study) `[well-documented]`; visual + on-screen-text + verbal hook all landing by ~1s `[well-documented]`; readable **sound-off** — roughly half of IG video is watched muted (Mosseri) `[well-documented]`. The sub-3s skip is a confirmed *negative* predictor `[platform-official]` — land the payoff/number/visual interrupt by ~frame 24–30.
- **Length:** emit BOTH a ≤15s loop micro-cut (rewatch lever) AND a 30–60s complete-idea cut; let his data pick the winner per niche. Don't hard-code a "magic" length — the "Mosseri reveals perfect Reel length" headlines have **no primary source** `[marketer-claim]`.

### CAROUSELS — the save/convert engine, his #1 untapped lever (target 2–3/week)
- **Which:** 8–12 slides (12–20 only for deep guides); cover → credibility → one-point-per-slide → dual CTA.
- **When:** turn every proven reel into a saveable "AI tool stack / money playbook"; reference content buyers bookmark before purchasing.
- **Why:** carousels generate **9× the saves** of single images (Metricool) `[well-documented]`, highest median engagement-rate-of-reach (Buffer 6.90%) `[well-documented]`, and get re-served when a viewer doesn't finish — multiple impressions per post `[well-documented]`. Explore explicitly weights likes, **saves, and shares** `[platform-official]`. **He runs ZERO today** `[platform-official — his stats.md]` — pure additive upside, no new filming, `carousel-builder` skill built and unused.
- **The gate:** slide 1 IS the hook (specificity, well under ~12 words); every body slide stands alone (re-serve resumes mid-deck, not at slide 1); ship BOTH a save CTA *and* a send CTA `[marketer-claim]`; add a swipe cue (only ~5% of carousels use one) `[marketer-claim]`.

### STORIES — the warm-lead / reply layer (daily, low-effort)
- **Which:** poll / Q&A / quiz stickers on AI-tool and money topics.
- **When:** daily retention touch — not a reach play.
- **Why:** Mosseri positions Stories for "friends, family, those closest" `[platform-official]`; Story *replies* rose 88% YoY (Metricool) `[well-documented]`. Job = convert existing followers into repliers/DMers, then route to the Broadcast Channel.

### LONG-FORM / YOUTUBE — the revenue + trust engine (the active pivot, 1–2/week)
- **Which:** **model-flood** ("I tested/built X with the newest model") = the compounding volume pillar; **build-in-public** = the membership pillar; **age-14 testimony** = flagship/identity anchor, NOT the volume engine.
- **When:** weekly pillar, then auto-clip into the week's reels (reverse funnel: short = cheap test, long = where subs + CPM live).
- **Why:** finance/business long-form RPM ~$10–29 vs ~$0.03–0.30 for the same content as a Short `[marketer-claim — ranking is durable]`; YouTube ranks on **valued (4–5-star) watch time**, not raw minutes `[platform-official]`; *recommendations drive more viewership than subscriptions or search* — every video earns its own reach `[platform-official]`. Title-first, then script `[well-documented]`. Audio quality is non-negotiable for the TV-screen shift `[well-documented]`.

### AUDIO as OFFENSE (not just the §7 constraint)
- "Go to the audio page" is one of the **four named Reels predictions** `[platform-official]`, and Meta's Transparency Center confirms audio-page taps AND "Use audio" taps are literal model inputs `[platform-official]`. Treat every voice-only reel as a *mintable, attributable* original-sound asset. Build 2–3 signature sonic hooks so external creators tapping "Use audio" fire a confirmed reuse signal back at his handle `[platform-official]`.
- **Decision rule:** default = API voice-only original-audio (fast, automatable, safe, loses ~nothing on the core watch-time/sends/likes signals). Override to a *manual in-app* trend-audio post ONLY when the sound itself is the discovery vector AND it's genuinely rising AND cleared for his account class `[platform-official constraint]`.

### COLLAB POSTS — the free reach multiplier (run 2–4/month)
- **Which:** co-authored Reel or carousel (NOT Stories — ineligible) `[platform-official]`.
- **Why:** dual-author collabs got ~2× impressions/interactions vs solo (Emplifi, 1.14M posts) `[well-documented]`; one post distributes to BOTH follower bases and pools early-engagement velocity into a single ranking event. **Larger account originates** `[well-documented — Mosseri]` — so Elijah originates when collabbing down (AI-educators like Berman, 49k IG, where he out-scales them), lets bigger accounts originate when reaching up.
