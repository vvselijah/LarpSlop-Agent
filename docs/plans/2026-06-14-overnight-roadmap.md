## How to read this

- **ADD-NOW** — clear value, low/known effort, composes with something already live, no Elijah decision needed to start. Build these.
- **ADD-LATER** — genuinely useful but an *enhancement* (the gap is already ~covered) OR gated on a real blocker (a second account, a paid scraper, a proven demand signal). Plan stays warm; build when the gate clears.
- **SKIP** — low value for *this* creator, or a paid product the hub already replicates free. Do not build; do not subscribe.

The hard constraint on everything: Windows + OneDrive-synced tree (no torch/cv2/heavy models on the synced disk), secrets only in env vars, official APIs over paid scrapers, and **Standing Rule 1 — never publish/post/DM without per-action confirmation.** Several of these candidates are *draft generators that stop before the publish click* — that is by design, not a limitation to "fix".
