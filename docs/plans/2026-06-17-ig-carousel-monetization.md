**The "5,000,000 views/month for 3 months" is a QUALIFICATION GATE to be *invited*, not a payout target.**

## 1. What this program actually is

- **Name:** *Instagram seasonal bonus for photo and carousel posts* — the carousel/photo variant of the
  **Bonuses on Instagram** program, which sits under Meta's broader **Content Monetization** umbrella (the
  2024–25 program that replaced the dead Reels Play Bonus).
- It is a **limited-time TEST**, **invite-only**, for select creators in **US, Japan, South Korea only**.
  Meta "reserves the right to update relevant terms" and can pause/end it at any time.
- It **genuinely pays on carousels AND single-image feed posts** — and a **video placed inside a carousel**
  also earns the post bonus. (Confirmed verbatim across 3 Meta-owned pages.) This is exactly your untapped format.
- **"Content Monetization"** is NOT a single payout you flip on — on these pages it's the *compliance policy*
  you must follow, not a product. Don't conflate the two.
- **Eligible ≠ invited ≠ earning.** Three stages: (1) clear the view gate → eligible; (2) Meta selects you →
  invitation banner/email in Professional Dashboard; (3) you **Activate bonus** + set up payout → you earn.

## 2. The 5M / 3-month requirement, decoded

- It is a **one-time eligibility/qualification gate**, region-tiered by trailing-3-consecutive-month views:
  **Japan 500K · South Korea 1M · US 5M** per month.
- It is **not** a payout floor, **not** a per-post tier, **not** an ongoing "keep earning" maintenance bar.
- **Your REELS reach is what cleared it** — a ~3.9M-view reel plus routine 100k–1M reels easily sums past
  5M/month, which is why you qualified despite posting ~zero carousels. (Meta doesn't *explicitly* say reel
  views count toward the carousel gate, but invites are account-level and both US thresholds are 5M, so this
  is the most likely read. Confirm in-app.)
- Hitting *exactly* 5M means you crossed the line to be **considered** — it guarantees no payout of any amount.

## 3. What it *really* pays

- Meta publishes **no fixed rate.** It shows an undisclosed **"Average RPM"** (rewards per 1,000 **qualified**
  views) that varies by viewer country, engagement, watch behavior, and advertiser demand.
- **Realistic 2026 Instagram band: ~$0.09–$0.50 per 1,000 qualified views (~$90–$500 per MILLION).** Your
  **Money/Finance (#1) and AI/Tech (#2)** niches sit at the **high end** (premium advertiser demand, US-heavy
  audience). Finance self-reports run as high as $0.30–$2.00+/1k.
- **Corrections to bad numbers floating around (verified false):**
  - The **$10–$50 per 1,000 views** figure is **TikTok / branded-deal** territory — wrong for IG by ~100–1000×.
  - **"Reels Play Bonus 2.0 / $0.12 cap"** references a **DEAD program** (Reels Play Bonus shut down **March 2023**).
  - A viral **"$2,000 on 358M views, $15,000 cap"** anecdote is a **dated Nov-2024 Reddit complaint** about a
    since-changed regime — directionally useful (payout decouples from views + is capped), not a current rate.
- **Qualified views ≠ headline views.** Viewer must be **logged in**; **profile-grid views don't count**; only
  certain screens (Explore/feed/Reels tab) qualify; a carousel most likely earns **one view per viewer on the
  first card, NOT per slide**; each post earns for up to **28 days**; only your **most recent 150 pieces** are
  eligible per 2-week bonus period. Net: payout-eligible views are **materially lower** than your headline numbers.
- **Per-deal CAP.** Earnings are capped per bonus deal (historical anecdotes cluster low-four to five figures/mo).
  Past the cap, extra views pay nothing — it is **not** uncapped views × RPM.

## 5. Best carousel strategies for YOUR niche (money/finance + AI)

You run **zero** carousels today → pure upside. Priority order:

1. **Repurpose proven reel hooks into carousels (low effort).** Don't invent topics — convert validated ones.
   Take a reel that already hit 100k–1M+, rebuild its script as a **6–8 slide carousel** (your `carousel-builder`
   skill), and post it **24–48h after the reel pops** to inherit the elevated ranking. Your disbelief openers
   ("ain't no way this AI makes $4k/mo", "absolutely unbelievable") become slide-1 hooks verbatim.
2. **Open-loop / "continue reading" structure (medium).** Pose the payoff on slide 1, withhold the answer until
   slide 5–6, plant a **second hook on slide 3.** Make slides 1, 3, 5 each a standalone scroll-stopper — carousels
   get re-surfaced *starting from different slides*, so multiple hooks = multiple reach attempts (a reel gets one).
3. **Lead with the 5 top money/AI formats (medium):** (a) **"Steal This"** template — "Steal my exact AI money
   prompt" (highest save rate); (b) **Comparison** — "ChatGPT vs Claude for making money" (most comments/debate);
   (c) **Listicle** — "7 AI tools that print money" (most consistent reach); (d) **Before/After** — "My income:
   month 1 vs month 6 using AI" (highest completion); (e) **Educational Breakdown** — "How the [X] system actually
   works" (authority).
4. **Engineer SAVES + SENDS, not raw impressions (low).** Make each topic DM-worthy and close with a value-framed
   "save for later / send to someone who needs this" — never as bait. Post **4:5 (1080×1350)** but keep all hook
   text + dollar figures inside the **center 1080×1080 square** so the cover reads cleanly on your grid.
5. **Ship 3–4 saves-optimized carousels/week; design is the moat (high).** Original layouts, substantive content
   per card, your voice on every slide — NOT 8 quote-graphics (those read as a prohibited "slideshow/text montage"
   and can't monetize even at high views). Frame money content **educationally**, not as scam shock-value.

## 6. Risks & disqualifiers (read before treating carousels as a money unlock)

- **Licensed/trending audio:** carousels *with* music ARE eligible (unlike API-published reels) — but music does
  NOT boost carousel payout, and over-reliance risks the same licensing limits. Hub rule stands: API-published
  content stays voice-only/original audio. Treat carousels as visual-first.
- **AI / unoriginal content:** AI-generated carousels monetize **only** with a meaningful original layer (your
  commentary/framing/analysis). Pure AI-stitched or rehosted slides risk demonetization as "unoriginal content."
- **Prohibited formats:** stacked static text-on-image = "slideshow of images"/"text montage" → can't monetize at
  any view count. Static image polls / static videos also barred. Use real design + substantive cards.
- **Engagement bait is a named disqualifier:** "comment YES", "tag 3 friends", "save or lose it" can make a post
  ineligible. Drive saves/sends through value; the ask must not be the post's purpose.
- **Restricted-category throttling:** your #1 Money/Finance niche brushes restricted topics (fraud, get-rich,
  scandal). Sensational framing can quietly reduce/disable monetization — keep it educational and uplifting.
- **Rate variability & cuts:** no fixed rate; Meta can change or **pause/cancel the program without notice.**
  Bonuses are historically unstable. **Screenshot today's in-app terms** as your source of truth.
- **Per-deal cap + minimum milestone:** read the cap and the minimum payout milestone on YOUR bonus screen —
  earnings below the milestone can be **forfeited**.
- **Excluded content:** branded, boosted/promoted, and collaborative posts do **not** earn. Keep bonus carousels
  non-branded and un-boosted.
- **US payout/tax:** set up a payout account + W-9 in-app the moment the invite appears (Meta issues a 1099 once
  reportable); payouts held until a minimum balance. Without a payout account you can bank up to $500/tool
  (max $1,500 across 3 tools) and forfeit after 6 months.
